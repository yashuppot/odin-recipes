Western Omelette

Description

A delicious and delectable classic Omelette. Cheesy, and topped with loads of bacon, mushroom, onion and ham.

Ingredients

  • Ham
  • Bacon
  • Egg (4 whole)
  • Cream (half cup)
  • Butter (1 Tbsp)

Steps

  1. Add butter to heated pan
  2. Beat eggs into cream
  3. Add ham and bacon to egg mixture
  4. Add to hot pan and cook until brown and solid
