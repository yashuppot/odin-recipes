Banana Shake

Description

A delicious and delectable banana smoothie. Chocolate and protein added to help you reach your fitness goals without sacrificing amazing taste!

Ingredients

  • Banana (1 whole)
  • Milk (1 cup)
  • Sugar (2 Tbsp)
  • Cocoa powder (1 Tsp)
  • Protein powder (2 scoops)

Steps

  1. Dice banana
  2. Add to blender along with milk, sugar, and protein
  3. Blend until smooth!
