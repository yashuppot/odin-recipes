Cheesecake

Description

A delicious and delectable classic New York Cheesecake. Soft and creamy cheese filling, enveloped in a smooth and crumbly, melt-in-your-mouth graham cracker crust.

Ingredients

  • Cream cheese (1 stick)
  • Milk (1 cup)
  • Sugar (1 cup)
  • Vanilla extract (1 Tsp)
  • Graham Crackers (1 pack)

Steps

  1. Mix cream cheese with milk and sugar
  2. Beat until smooth
  3. Chill
  4. Mix crushed graham crackers with water and brown sugar
  5. Spread on bottom of foil pan, add chilled cheese mixture, and bake at 350 for 30 minutes
